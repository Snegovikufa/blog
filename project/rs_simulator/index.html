MiT Office: WAG

Rustam Safin Jan 10, 2016

When I worked in “MiT-Ufa” LLC one of our client asked to develop a special working tool for calculating levels of production and injection in the reservoirs with the full WAG. With customer’s engineers and our teammates we built a desktop C# application that simulates simplified reservoir and surface equipment behaviour. We used Windows Presentation Foundation to implement UI.

My responsibilities:

  • Software requirements preparation
  • Worked with customers requirements
  • Managed development team
  • Development and support
  • Continuous integration
  • Reports preparation

Technology stack used

  • C#
  • WPF
  • LINQ
  • XML
